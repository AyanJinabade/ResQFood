Project Overview

Resqfood is a web-based food donation platform designed to reduce food wastage by connecting surplus food providers (restaurants, households) with NGOs and volunteers to enable timely and efficient food redistribution.

Problem Statement

Large quantities of edible food are wasted daily due to the lack of a structured system connecting food donors with NGOs. Resqfood aims to bridge this gap by providing a centralized, easy-to-use digital platform.

Solution

Enables food providers to list surplus food details

Allows NGOs to view, request, and manage food donations

Streamlines communication between donors and NGOs

Promotes sustainability and social responsibility

Key Features

User-friendly and responsive UI

Food donation listing and management

NGO request and coordination workflow

Real-time availability of surplus food

Scalable frontend architecture
